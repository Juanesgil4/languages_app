# Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started in 3 Steps

### 1. Open the App
Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

### 2. Choose Your Mode

#### Conversation Practice
- Click on a topic (Sport, Viaggi, Identità Personale, Opinioni)
- Select difficulty (Facile/Medio/Difficile) - this sets the timer
- Select answer type (Breve/Elaborata/Entrambe) - except for Opinions
- Practice speaking out loud!
- Click "Suggerimento" for hints
- Click "Vedi Risposta" to see example answers
- Click "Prossima" for next question

#### Grammar Drills
- Click on a grammar category (Aggettivi, Articoli, Verbi, etc.)
- Select a specific drill
- Type your answer in the blank
- Press Enter or click "Controlla" to check
- Click "Regole" to see grammar rules
- Click "Prossima" for next exercise

### 3. Practice!
- Speak out loud for conversation mode
- Type answers for drill mode
- Use the timer to challenge yourself
- Review hints and answers to learn

## Tips for Best Results

### Conversation Mode
1. **Read the question out loud**
2. **Think in Italian** (don't translate from your native language)
3. **Use the timer** to simulate real conversation pressure
4. **Try short answers first** for quick practice
5. **Try long answers** to build fluency
6. **Check hints** if you're stuck on structure
7. **Review example answers** to learn new phrases

### Grammar Drills
1. **Read the rules first** (click "Regole")
2. **Type your answer** in the blank
3. **Don't worry about mistakes** - they help you learn!
4. **Review the correct answer** even if you got it right
5. **Repeat drills** until they become automatic

## Difficulty Levels

- **Facile (Easy)**: 30 seconds - Good for beginners
- **Medio (Medium)**: 20 seconds - Good for intermediate
- **Difficile (Hard)**: 10 seconds - Good for advanced/fluent

## Answer Types (Conversation Only)

- **Breve (Short)**: 1-3 words, quick responses
- **Elaborata (Long)**: Full sentences with context
- **Entrambe (Both)**: Mix of short and long answers

## Troubleshooting

### The app doesn't load
- Make sure all files are in the correct folders
- Check browser console for errors (F12)
- Make sure JavaScript is enabled

### Questions don't appear
- Check that `data/questions.js` is loaded
- Open browser console to see errors

### Drills don't work
- Check that `data/drills-data.js` and `data/drills-metadata.js` are loaded
- Make sure the drill ID matches in both files
