When users click on the **Preposizioni** button, they will see:

### 1. Preposizioni Semplici (03_01)
- Practice with simple prepositions: DI, A, DA, IN, CON, SU, PER, TRA/FRA
- 100 exercises in the source file
- 20 exercises in the app

### 2. Preposizioni Articolate (03_02) ✨ NEW!
- Practice combining prepositions with articles
- Learn contractions: del, al, dal, nel, sul, etc.
- Complete contraction table for all forms
- 20 interactive exercises

## How to Use

1. **Open** `index.html` in your browser
